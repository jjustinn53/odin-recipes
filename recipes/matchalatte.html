Matcha Latte

Description

A smooth and creamy blend of vibrant green matcha, frothed milk, and a touch of sweetness. This antioxidant-rich drink offers a gentle energy boost and a perfect balance of earthy and velvety flavors. Enjoy it hot or iced!

Ingredients

  • 6 grams of matcha (Marukyu Koyamaen Preferably)
  • Oatmilk
  • Raw Organic Unfiltered Honey

Directions

  1. Sift Matcha – Add 1 teaspoon of matcha powder to a bowl and sift to remove clumps.
  2. Whisk with Water – Pour 2 ounces (about ¼ cup) of hot water (175°F/80°C) over the matcha and whisk in a zigzag motion until frothy.
  3. Heat Milk – Warm 6 ounces (¾ cup) of milk in a saucepan or froth it using a milk frother. For an iced latte, use cold milk.
  4. Sweeten (Optional) – Stir in honey, maple syrup, or sugar to taste.
  5. Assemble – Pour the whisked matcha into a cup, add the frothed milk, and stir gently.
  6. Serve & Enjoy – Top with extra foam or a sprinkle of matcha powder. Enjoy hot or iced!
